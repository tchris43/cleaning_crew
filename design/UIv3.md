# UI Specification v3 - Visual Hierarchy & Branding Updates

## Critical Design Goal

The website should feel like a digital version of the flyer.

The flyer is not merely a branding reference.

The flyer is the primary visual and layout reference.

When choosing between:

* Modern website conventions
* Matching the flyer

Choose the flyer.

---

# Visual Hierarchy

Users should immediately understand:

1. What services are offered
2. What they cost
3. How to book

The website should not behave like a marketing landing page.

The website should behave like a digital booking brochure.

---

# Updated Page Order

HEADER

DISCOUNT BANNER

SERVICE PACKAGES

BOOK APPOINTMENT

VEHICLE SIZE GUIDE

FAQ

CONTACT

The booking form should appear after the service packages.

Users should see pricing before booking.

---

# Remove Generic Marketing Sections

Remove entirely:

* Why customers book here
* Local service, simple booking
* Trust badges
* Marketing value propositions
* Generic sales copy

Most visitors already received:

* A flyer
* A referral
* A text message

The website does not need to sell the service.

The website needs to facilitate booking.

---

# Logo Requirements

The flyer logo should be used exactly.

Requirements:

* Use the actual logo asset from the flyer
* Preserve bubble mark
* Preserve logo proportions
* Do not recreate with plain text
* Do not replace with generated typography

The logo should be one of the most visually prominent elements on the page.

Header logo height:

Approximately 60–80px.

---

#FFFFFF

# Service Package Cards

The service package cards should be the most visually prominent section of the website.

Goal:

Approximately 30-40% of above-the-fold visual attention.

---

## Layout

Desktop:

Three cards side-by-side.

Mobile:

Stacked vertically.

Cards should resemble the flyer.

Not generic SaaS pricing cards.

---

## Card Styling

Background:

White

Border:

2-3px solid #20263F

Corner Radius:

8px

Padding:

Generous

No large shadows.

---

## Deep Clean Card

The Deep Clean card should be emphasized.

Include:

Most Popular

badge.

Badge Background:

#D9A62E

Badge Text:

#20263F

# Flyer Content Preservation

Service cards should contain the actual flyer content.

Do not summarize.

Do not rewrite.

Include:

* Descriptions
* Included services
* Small pricing
* Mid-sized pricing
* Large pricing

The flyer content is authoritative.

---

# Booking Section Redesign

The booking form should not dominate the page visually.

The packages are the primary decision-making element.

The booking form is the action taken after the decision.

---

[9:30 AM]
[10:00 AM]

Selected state:

Background:

#20263F

Text:

Unselected state:

Border:

---

# Bubble Motif

The current implementation does not use enough flyer branding.

Use the bubble pattern from the flyer.

Locations:

* Hero background
* Footer background

Opacity:

5-10%

The bubbles should be decorative only.

Never interfere with readability.

---

# Whitespace Rules

Avoid:

Large empty sections.

Avoid:

Blank containers.

Avoid:

Unused columns.

Every major section should contain meaningful content.

---

# Visual Weight Targets

Most visual emphasis:

1. Logo
2. Service Package Cards
3. Book Appointment Button
4. Booking Form
5. Everything Else

The service package cards should attract more attention than the booking form.

---

# Final Design Test

Before approving a design ask:

"If I removed the logo, would this still look obviously related to the flyer?"

If the answer is no:

The design is not aligned enough with the flyer.

Increase flyer influence until the connection is obvious.
